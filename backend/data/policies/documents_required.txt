Required Documents:

Students must submit the following documents for accommodation:

Mandatory Documents:
- Aadhaar card (original + photocopy)
- College ID card (valid student ID)
- Passport-size photographs (4 copies)
- Parents/Guardian contact details
- Local emergency contact information

Additional Requirements:
- Police verification may be required for some accommodations
- Income certificate for scholarship students
- Medical fitness certificate (for some hostels)
- No objection certificate from college (if required)

Processing Time:
- Document verification: 1-2 business days
- Police verification (if required): 3-7 business days
