Smoking Policy:

Smoking is allowed only in designated smoking-friendly accommodations.
Smoking in non-smoking PGs may lead to penalties.

Rules for smoking accommodations:
- Smoking only allowed in designated areas
- No smoking in common areas or shared spaces
- Proper ventilation must be maintained
- Fire safety protocols must be followed

Violations in smoke-free accommodations may result in:
- Immediate penalty charges
- Cleaning fees for odor removal
- Possible eviction for repeated violations
